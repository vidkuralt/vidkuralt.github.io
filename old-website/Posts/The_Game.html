It is only a demo and will not receive any more updates!

The game is simply just called The Game. You are playing in 1st person and your goal is to shoot all the targets. The game has unfortunately only 3 levels. To make things a little more interesting you are competing against time! Controls: you move with WSAD (or arrow keys), space for jump, with mouse you move your look, left click you shoot a bullet.

Compatible with: Windows 7/8/10

Completed: Well kinda

Created with (in) Unity
