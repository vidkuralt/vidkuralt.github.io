The Logo



The idea for this just appeared before my eyes when I was fiddling with different tools in Adobe's Photoshop (program designed for image editing, retouching, creating image compositions, website mockups, and adding effects). Originally I just meant to reuse the same photo I use for social media, but this new logo changed my mind. The logo represents letters V and I, which stands for Vid. Both letters together form a letter K, which stands for Kuralt.
