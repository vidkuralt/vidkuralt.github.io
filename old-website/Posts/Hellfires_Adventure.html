For this game, you need up to date Java.

TYPE: ARCADE GAME

FILE: .jar

RECOMMENDED: Version 8 Update 181 or higher

DESCRIPTION: THIS IS MY FIRST Arcade game. It was coded in Java. You play as a frog. Your goal is to eat all flies to progress to the next level. But watch out for your natural enemies - storks. Controls: frog moves automatically; right arrow - clockwise; left arrow - anticlockwise; down arrow - hold; up arrow - speed.
