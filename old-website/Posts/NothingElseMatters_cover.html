Nothing Else Matters Cover



Here you can listen (or you are already listening) to my latest guitar project. I have published this song on SoundCloud and YouTube. The story behind the Youtube video: we have awaited the destruction of our galaxy Milky Way as our Sun turned itself into the Black Hole and stared to suck everything. But no one believed the scientists, so our galaxy was not evacuated until it was too late. Only a small group of fearless individuals decided to listen to those tariffing inevitable forecasts, escaped the Milky Way, and went on an adventure to find a new home. Will they be successful? Enjoy and “relax” while watching this space odyssey and/or listening to the cover of Nothing Else Matters performed by me.
